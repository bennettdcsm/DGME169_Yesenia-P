Professional Opportunities

I’m open to collaborations, internships, research roles, and projects related to embedded systems, robotics, and medical technology.

For Employers & Collaborators

I am especially interested in roles that involve embedded systems, microcontroller programming, robotics, and tools for healthcare or neuroscience. I enjoy working on cross-functional teams where hardware, software, and design come together.

Services & Collaboration

  • Embedded firmware development for ESP32 and similar microcontrollers.
  • Sensor integration and communication protocols (I2C, SPI, UART, BLE).
  • Rapid prototyping for robotics and interactive hardware.
  • Python tools for data processing, image analysis, or lab workflows.
  • Workshops or small group sessions on microcontroller basics.

Sponsorship & Support (Optional)

I also run embedded systems workshops for students. Support for hardware, boards, or lab materials helps more students get hands-on experience with engineering. If you are interested in supporting these efforts, please reach out through the contact page.
